This project focused on building a mini-app for **K–12 students** to better organize and access mathematical knowledge.

I worked as a **Co-Designer** and **Co-Developer** on a system that helps students manage learning resources such as chapter summaries, formulas and theorems, geometric models, mathematical ideas, and test materials.

The project also introduced a **data matching algorithm** to improve the user experience of data querying and formula invocation. In addition to the technical design, the project was used in support of a **computer software copyright registration** application.

**Tools:** software design, educational technology, data matching, product development
