This project summarizes my research experience in **scientific machine learning**, with a focus on **medical image segmentation**, **structured pruning**, and **neural operators for physical systems**.

At the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, I studied U-Net and U-Net++ architectures and implemented deep learning pipelines in **PyTorch**. I designed **CPG (Channel Pruning with Grouping)**, a DFS-guided structured pruning method for efficient medical image segmentation, achieving high accuracy with reduced computational cost.

I also co-developed **MM-NO (Multi-Modal Neural Operator)** for learning physical operators from heterogeneous data. This work combined dense field modeling with sparse sensor representations and was evaluated on **Navier–Stokes** and **Shallow Water PDE** benchmarks.

**Tools:** PyTorch, Python, neural operators, PDE modeling, deep learning
